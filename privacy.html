Privacy Policy

InstaType is a paid Chrome extension that uses the Google Docs API to provide smart auto-typing functionality. In order to function, InstaType requires access to your Google account to interact with your documents.

We respect your privacy. InstaType does not collect or store your location data, browsing history, or any unrelated personal information. We only access what is needed to provide the typing functionality.

By using InstaType, you consent to this access solely for the purpose of enabling its core features.
